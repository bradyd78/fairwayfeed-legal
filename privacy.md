# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 1, 2026

Bad Apps LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates the Fairway Feed mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

## Information We Collect

**Account information:** When you sign up, we collect your email address and a display name you choose.

**Content you create:** Posts, photos, group memberships, chat messages, and stats (handicap, best score, holes played) you enter or generate in the App.

**Technical information:** Device type and version of the App you're using, collected automatically by our backend provider (Firebase) to help us diagnose issues.

We do **not** collect location data, contacts, biometric data, browsing history outside the App, or any tracking identifiers for advertising purposes.

## How We Use Information

We use the information you provide to:
- Operate the App (sign you in, show your posts to your groups, build the leaderboard)
- Respond to user reports and moderate inappropriate content
- Communicate with you about your account if needed
- Improve and debug the App

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers.

## Data Storage and Processing

The App uses Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage) as its backend. Your data is stored on Google's servers, which are governed by Google's privacy and security practices. See https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy for details.

## Sharing With Other Users

Content you post to a group is visible to other members of that group. Chat messages in a group are visible to other members of that group. Your display name and stats are visible on group leaderboards.

You can block any other user, which hides their content from you.

## Your Rights

- **Access:** You can see all of your data within the App itself.
- **Correction:** You can edit your display name, stats, and posts in the App.
- **Deletion:** You can permanently delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete Account. This removes your account, posts, group memberships, and stats. Some data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being purged.
- **California, EU, and UK residents:** You have additional rights under CCPA, GDPR, and UK GDPR including the right to request a copy of your data, restrict processing, or lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Contact us at the email below to exercise these rights.
